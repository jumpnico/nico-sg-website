Your Questions Answered

What makes Imabari towels special?

Imabari towels are renowned for their exceptional softness and absorbency, a result of meticulous Japanese craftsmanship.

How to best enjoy Japanese brandy?

Japanese brandy can be savored neat, on the rocks, or in sophisticated cocktails. Its complex notes offer a unique tasting experience. We secretly enjoy putting a few drops into coffee or vanilla ice cream.

Are the body care products all-natural?

Our strawberry body care products feature high-quality ingredients, 90% or more naturally derived for a refreshing and gentle experience.
